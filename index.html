Hotel Aviva ****

Located in Resita, Hotel Aviva offers free WiFi, free air conditioning, a 24-hour front desk, a wellness area and a restaurant.
Private parking spaces are available on site free of charge and the closest bus stop is 100 m away.
The rooms at the Hotel Aviva all come with a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a minibar and a laptop-size safety deposit box. In addition, private bathroom facilities are available, as well.
Breakfast is served daily at the property and dinner and drinks can be enjoyed at the in-house restaurant and bar. The closest grocery shop is 100 m away.
The wellness area comprises a sauna, a hot tub and a fitness centre. Guests can also take advantage of a luggage storage room and a car hire.
Families particularly like the location — they rated it 9.1 for a stay with kids.
