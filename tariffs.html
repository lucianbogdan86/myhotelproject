Tariff Terms and Conditions

Children

When sharing rooms with two adults, children 4 years & under stay free of charge on our Bed and Breakfast tariff (For Dinner, Bed & Breakfast a Children's Menu supplement may be applicable). When sharing with two adults, for children 5 - 11 years there is a daily charge of €12.00 Bed and Breakfast or €20.00 for 12-15 year-olds. Many of our double or twin rooms take may take an extra bed, please get in contact to find out more. Children staying in their own room are charged at 50% of room rate (Bed & Breakfast tariff), except during Folk Week (29 July- 5th August) and September when full terms will be charged.

Rooms

All advertised terms are per room inclusive of VAT at the current rate gratuities are left to the discretion of guests. Dedicated Single rooms are charged with no supplement. All rooms have a private en suite with bath or shower - flat-screen TV Free-view with radio - direct dial telephone - tea making facilities. We can only be responsible for valuables that have been receipted at reception.

Wifi

Free Wifi is available in the hotel public areas and in rooms in the Jubilee wing.

Parking

All parking is at visitors own risk. There are 30 spaces in our car park.

Dining

Our Restaurant offers a 4-course set menu with choices. It is recommended that guests book Dinner in advance to avoid disappointment. A Children's Menu is available but must be pre-booked. Bar Snacks are available in the Gold Bar 12-2pm ; Packed Lunches are available to book, please ask for more information. There is no reduction for meals not taken by guests with a Dinner, Bed & Breakfast tariff reservation.

Dogs

We welcome well-behaved dogs to stay in our rooms, at a supplement charge of €10 per day. Please note however that dogs are not allowed in the Drawing Room, Bar or Dining Room. There are rooms with easy access, or direct access to outside which can be very convenient for dog-owner, please contact us to find out more. Dogs are only allowed on the main beach from October to April inclusive.

Smoking

The Hotel operates a non-smoking policy throughout.

Reservation Fee

€50.00 per person is requested upon making a reservation, this will be credited to the account. The reservation fee is non-refundable unless the room is re-let, our cancellation policy is to charge up to two-thirds of the cost. Please insure your holiday, details will be sent on confirmation. This policy will be reviewed in line with the latest government guidelines. A €50 per person deposit is required at the time of booking. The deposit is non-refundable against cancellation, except if covered by insurance or cancelled 12 weeks in advance when the deposit may be refunded less €10 administration fee. Cancellation within 12 weeks will be charged 2/3 of total.

The terms quoted are subject to amendment, should rates have to be adjusted at any time guests would be notified immediately. The hotel reserves the right to cancel a booking without liability in any event beyond its control. In such an event the hotel will refund the deposit but will have no further liability to the guest.
